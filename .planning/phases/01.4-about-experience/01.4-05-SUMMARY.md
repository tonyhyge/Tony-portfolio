## Key Technical Details

### DualTrackGroup Component
- **Desktop layout:** `grid-cols-[1fr_auto_1fr]` — industry track (left), center line, research track (right)
- **Mobile layout:** `flex flex-col gap-4` — stacked with track badges
- **Center line:** `w-px bg-accent self-stretch` spans full row height, `aria-hidden="true"`
- **Track badges:** `Badge variant="outline"` with `md:hidden` — mobile-only
- **hideLine:** Both ExperienceEntries receive `hideLine` — no individual dots/connectors
- **Bottom connector:** 1.5rem `w-px bg-border` between groups, hidden for last
- **Single-entry backstop:** Logs `console.warn` and renders as single-column

### groupExperiences() Function
- Single-pass O(n) Map-based grouping
- Entries with `groupId` → grouped into arrays
- Entries without `groupId` → singles
- Combined array sorted by `startDate` descending (reverse chronological)
- Module-private (not exported), lives inline per D-17

### Timeline Rendering
- `Array.isArray(item)` branches between DualTrackGroup and ExperienceEntry
- `isLast = i === grouped.length - 1` correctly handles groups and singles
- Result: Group [AI Engineer + Research Assistant] rendered as dual-track, then Research Intern as single-entry
