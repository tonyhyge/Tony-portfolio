    1. **D-02 — 90vh height:** `min-h-[90vh]` on the section with `flex-col items-center justify-center` vertically centering all content. Per UI-SPEC.md "Spacing Exceptions": "Hero section height: min-h-[90vh] (D-02 in CONTEXT.md) — viewport-relative, not additive spacing scale."

    2. **D-01 — Split layout:** The inner flex div uses `flex items-start justify-between` on mobile (hexagon aligns to top of text) and `md:items-center` on desktop (hexagon centers vertically). Gap uses `gap-8` (32px) mobile, `md:gap-12` (48px), `lg:gap-16` (64px) per UI-SPEC.md spacing scale (xl = 32px, 2xl = 48px, 3xl = 64px). Text block limited to `max-w-2xl` (672px max-width) to prevent excessive line width on ultra-wide screens.

    3. **D-03/D-15 — Name with gradient text:** `<h1>` with "Minh Pham" uses `bg-gradient-to-r from-[var(--accent)] to-[var(--accent-secondary)] bg-clip-text text-transparent` for cyan-to-violet gradient text effect per D-15. `text-4xl md:text-5xl font-semibold leading-[1.1]` per UI-SPEC.md Heading role: "48px / clamp 40-56px (text-4xl md:text-5xl), weight 600 (semibold), line-height 1.1."

    4. **D-04 — Tagline:** `<p>` with "AI Engineer &amp; Researcher" directly below the h1. `text-base text-foreground` per UI-SPEC.md Typography Body role (16px, weight 400). JSX requires `&amp;` for the ampersand character.

    5. **D-05 — No intro paragraph:** Text block contains exactly h1 + p + HeroCtas + ScrollIndicator(mobile). No additional introductory text.

    6. **D-07 — Hexagon positioning:** HexagonBadge always visible (`shrink-0`). Default sizing from HexagonBadge: 64px mobile, 120px at md:+
    (from Plan 01's `size-[64px] md:size-[120px]`). Top margin of `mt-2` provides a small gap from the top of the text on mobile since `items-start` alignment puts hexagon at text top; hexagon may be visually higher than the h1 without this. Reset on desktop via `md:mt-0`.

    7. **ScrollIndicator placement — Two locations via responsive visibility:**

       - Desktop (`hidden md:absolute md:bottom-8 md:flex`): Absolutely positioned at the bottom center of the hero section. Visible only when the hero has full 90vh space to justify a bottom indicator.
       Follows D-11 (scroll indicator at bottom of hero) — the absolute positioning on desktop places it at the bottom regardless of content height.
