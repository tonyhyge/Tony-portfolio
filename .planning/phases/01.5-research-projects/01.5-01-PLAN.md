       - endDate: z.string().optional()
       - venue: z.string().optional()
       - collaborators: z.array(z.string()).optional()
       - featured: z.boolean().default(false)
       - thumbnail: z.string().optional()

    4. Derive `export type ResearchProject = z.infer<typeof ResearchProjectSchema>`.

    5. Define a `const projectData` array of 5 sample ResearchProject-shaped objects matching Minh's background (AI Engineer + NLP/ML research):
